Hi my name is Olivia and I am a VCD major and Art minor here at EWU. This is my fifth and final year here. I really love to draw cartoons and hopefully someday I'll be able to do is as a job. I am excited to graduate and start my life outside of school.


Running with Scissors is a memoir by Augusten Burroughs. The way Augusten shares his experiences makes this book so enjoyable.

Cheesecake, especially strawberry cheesecake, is my favorite desert. It is so creamy and fruity. It just is the perfect desert after any meal.

Lobster is my favorite food to eat if I have money to spend. It can be a bit pricey but the taste is so worth it. Dipped in melted butter, lobster it so tender and juicy. Thinking about the flavor makes my mouth water.

Sushi is another favorite that can get a little pricey. I really love seafood and with sushi it allows you to taste all sorts of different fish paired with vegetables. If you are not in the mood to pick one plate to eat, a sushi bar can be the perfect place to go out.
